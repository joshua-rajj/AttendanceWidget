## What is Attendance Widget?

**Attendance Widget** is an Android home screen widget that shows:

-  **Current class**
-  **Next class**
-  **Real-time attendance status for each class**
-  Daily timetable view
-  Quick refresh support

Instead of opening multiple pages inside an app, everything you need is available **directly on your home screen**.

---

##  The Problem It Solves

You're not the only one who faced this.


During attendance:
- The professor might **miss your roll number**
- You might not hear your name
- Or there could be confusion during marking

The usual process to verify attendance:

    Open PESU Academy:   
    → Unlock using biometrics 
    → Go to Attendance
    → Select Course
    → Check Date
    → Verify status


It’s tedious.

And what usually happens?

- You feel lazy to check immediately.
- You forget.
- One week later you randomly check your attendance.
- You realize you were marked absent.
- Now it’s too late to request a correction.

That one missed attendance slowly drops your percentage.

---

##  How Attendance Widget Fixes This


-  Attendance status is visible instantly on your home screen.
-  You can check right after attendance using the quick refresh feature.
-  You can request the professor to correct it then and there.


---


##  Goal

To ensure no student ever says:

> "Shit man.. I missed my attendance"
